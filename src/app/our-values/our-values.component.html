OUR VALUES
A dish is worth a thousands words
Authenticity
Italian culinary traditions are sacred, and we are committed to being consistent with the recipes handed down by our grandmothers. We do not propose refined cuisine but the warmest and most welcoming home cooking, just like a grandmother's.
Generosity
Who is more generous than a grandmother? The one who shares knowledge and food, with generous portions, selflessness and spirit of sacrifice dedicated to family. We want to be like them: optimistic, idealistic, inclusive with anyone who will sit at our table.
Community
Building a community passionate about Italian cuisine and culture, investing the earnings to make physical goods and exclusive content, is our way of opening doors to the world of Italian grandmothers, allowing you to join a big family.
